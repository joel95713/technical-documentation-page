Civilizations

Explore the unique traits and strengths of each civilization. From the English to the Mongols, each civilization brings distinct advantages to the battlefield. Master their abilities and lead your chosen nation to dominance.

advantage = getAdvantage("Mongols");

In Age of Empires 4, civilizations are more than just cosmetic choices. Each civilization has its own set of unique bonuses, units, and technologies that reflect the historical strengths of that nation. Whether it's the English longbowmen, the Mongol horse archers, or the imposing Teutonic Knights, your chosen civilization will significantly influence your gameplay strategy.

To truly master a civilization, it's essential to understand its strengths and weaknesses. While some civilizations might excel in rapid expansion, others might focus on defensive structures or powerful siege weaponry. As you explore and experiment with different civilizations, you'll uncover the intricacies of their playstyles and develop strategies that suit your preferences.

Remember, the advantage you gain from each civilization goes beyond raw power. It's about utilizing their distinct features to adapt to various challenges on the battlefield, outsmarting your opponents, and leading your nation to dominance in the ever-changing world of Age of Empires 4.
